This is a faithful, accessible digital edition of the 1994 report, rebuilt from a 112-page print-locked scan. It conforms to WCAG 2.1 Level AA; the PDF additionally targets PDF/UA-1. About this edition

Every figure leads with its accessible form — a descriptive caption, a data table, and an interactive or vector chart that names the figure it makes accessible (for example, “Figure 1-1 — accessible diagram”). The original 1994 scan sits in a collapsible “Show the original 1994 scan” accordion so readers can verify the accessible version against the source. No data, text, or figures were altered, modernized, or invented; printed-era values are preserved exactly as published.

Source code, the build pipeline, and release downloads are on GitHub.

Originally published by the Violence Prevention Research Program, University of California, Davis (1994). This accessible edition reproduces the source faithfully for assistive-technology users; copyright in the underlying text remains with the original author and publisher.
